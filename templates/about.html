Who We Are

FitIntel was founded in 2023 with a mission to make personalized health and fitness guidance accessible to everyone. Our team of nutritionists, fitness experts, and AI specialists work together to create intelligent systems that adapt to your unique needs.

We believe that one-size-fits-all approaches to health don't work. That's why we've developed sophisticated algorithms that consider your body type, health conditions, fitness level, and personal preferences to deliver recommendations that actually work for you.

Our platform combines the latest research in nutrition science, exercise physiology, and machine learning to provide you with the most effective and sustainable path to better health.
