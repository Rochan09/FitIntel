Health Guidelines

  • Meal timing: Eat meals at regular times each day to help control blood sugar levels.
  • Portion control: Use the plate method (½ plate vegetables, ¼ plate protein, ¼ plate carbs).
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Physical activity: Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate activity most days.
  • Regular monitoring: Check your blood glucose levels as recommended by your healthcare provider.
